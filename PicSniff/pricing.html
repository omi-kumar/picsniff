Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free account work?

The Picsniff free account allows you to host more than 9000+ photos with additional features like Unlimited Galleries, Video Integration, Liveness detection, Face recognition for upto 200 search and Pre-registeration for upto 20 guests

Can I switch plans?

Yes, you can switch plans. You can get in touch with us for the same.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes, with the professional plan you can use a custom domain to share with your clients. We also provide picsniff.com domain to all the accounts on signup

Do you offer discounts for yearly plan?

Yes, you can save 16% if you take the yearly plan.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at anytime from your account dashboard

What type of payments do you support?

We currently accept Visa, MasterCard credit and debit cards, as well as American Express credit cards. All payments are billed in USD.
